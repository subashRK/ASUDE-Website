Why ASUDE?

ASUDE game increases attention and concentration, reduces stress and improves pro-social behaviors such as sharing and complimenting or helping others.

ASUDE therapeutic intervention can improve social, emotional, and psychological well-being. We assess the person through the board game. Though there are many board games in the world, ASUDE is unique as it encourages positive thinking, which in turn promotes healthy human mind.

About Us

ASUDE game is a complete tool designed to transform life in a beautiful way, learn the art of living in a simple way and implement it in your life to achieve success and achieve a peaceful and happy mental state. It is the world's first social thinking game.

Asude game was invented by U. Abdul Rahman in 2018 and received two Copyright certificates from the Government of India. Asude game has 1000+ levels and can be played by 40 people at the same time. Asude game is advanced and helps people in many ways such as therapy, competition, life development, training, etc. ASUDE Game provides all types of holistic training to schools, colleges, industries, NGOs, companies and any individual who strives to improve themselves.
